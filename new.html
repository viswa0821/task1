About Star Wars

Star Wars is a groundbreaking science fiction franchise created by George Lucas. It introduced the world to iconic characters, planets, and a storyline that spans generations. From its first film in 1977 to the latest installments, Star Wars has had a significant impact on pop culture and the film industry.
